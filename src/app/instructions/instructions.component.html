Game

  • Game Types : 3 minute game and 5 minutes game
    • 3 minute : You have total of 180 seconds to win/draw. If you fails to complete in time you loose
    • 5 minute : You have total of 300 seconds to win/draw. If you fails to complete in time you loose
  • Player types: Random player, Friend and AI
    • Random : Play against a random online player
    • Friend : Play against your friend (You have to login to play against your friend)
    • AI : Play against the artificial intelligence.
  • Points : 10 points for a win, 0 points for draw and -10 points for a lose. If you exit the game without completing, 20 points will be deducted to ensure a smooth game

How to play?

  • Board is divided into 9 sub boards
  • Say, if you play on 1st sub board 3rd position, your opponent has to play on the 3rd sub board. Means you control your opponents play!!
  • If you win 3 sub board horizontally, vertically or diagonally, you win the game.
  • If opponent ran out of time, you win and vice versa
  • Hints can be used throughout the game. It shows you the next best move. To get hints, you have to watch a video from the dashboard
